WHO WE ARE

IMPACT.T (Technology Hub) is a technology hub, a social institution for startups.

Mission - 1

This is not just a coworking space, but a technological hub for people who are building the companies of the future. Here, enterprising people will be able to find answers to their questions, consult with other specialists and develop.

Mission - 2

We want to hold conferences and educational meetings about programming, digital technologies, marketing and future business. And also that the founders and leaders of the IT-sphere give free advice and consultations to our residents.

Mission - 3

We plan to create a community where people help each other create useful companies and products. Where people share experience and knowledge. We not only provide jobs for our residents, but also invite specialists and develop new communities.
